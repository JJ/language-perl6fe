<p>A more colorful, thoughtful, and helpful language grammar for Perl 6. Derived from the builtin <a href="https://github.com/atom/language-perl">language-perl</a> package but with many bugfixes and additions.  You might ask why I didn't just add a PR there but I feel that this package adds quite a bit more that it might not be everyone's cup of tea. However, I do plan on adding support for autocomplete+, linter, atom-build, and atom-runner.  Maybe even integrating a perl6 REPL would be fun!</p>

<p><a href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MadcapJake/language-perl6fe/master/images/example3.png" target="_blank"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MadcapJake/language-perl6fe/master/images/example3.png" alt="A screenshot of an funnified Perl 6 file" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>

<h2><a id="user-content-how-do-i-use-this" class="anchor" href="#how-do-i-use-this" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>How do I use this?</h2>

<p>You have three options:</p>

<ol>
<li>Click the language name in the status-bar (<code>Ctrl+Shift+L</code>) and select <code>Perl 6 FE</code></li>
<li>Disable or uninstall the <code>language-perl</code> package</li>
<li><p>Add this to your <code>config.cson</code> (<em>Application: Open Your Config</em>):</p>

<div class="highlight highlight-source-coffee"><pre><span class="pl-s"><span class="pl-pds">'</span>*<span class="pl-pds">'</span></span><span class="pl-k">:</span>
